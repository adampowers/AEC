Our story starts with one man's discontent. Say hello to Joe. [Picture of joe] Joe moved to San Diego in the early 80's and quickly fell in love with the sunshine, the surf and the amazing Mexican food. One thing Joe didn't like was his job. After 15-years of being a desk-jockey working for a local Big-Corp, Joe decided it was time to change. So in 1997, long before everybody and his second cousin started a food truck, Joe took to the streets (yeah, he's kinda hip like that). Joe maintains a commitment to quality ingredients and an authentic San Diego take on tacos. That means a lot of fresh seafood. Be assured when you come to Joe's, you're getting the best tacos out there. Joe likes to move around, opening up shop in a different location every day of the week. You can check the map and calendar below for where he's going to be today. See you on the streets.
