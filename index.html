Our story starts with one man's passion for great food. Say hello to Pepe. Pepe moved to San Diego in the early 80's and fell in love with the sunshine, the surf and the amazing food, particularly of the Mexican variety. One thing Pepe didn't love was his job. After a few years of working at a local tech company, Pepe had had enough and decided it was time to hit the streets, literally! So in the summer of 2004, Pepe's Tacos was born.

Pepe is committed to using quality ingredients and has uniquely San Diego take on tacos. That means a lot of seafood and a lot of avocado. Pepe gets his ingredients fresh each morning so you can rest assured that when you visit Pepe, you're getting the best tacos in SD.

Pepe likes to move around, opening up shop in a different locations throughout the week. Check out the map and calendar below to see where he's going to be today. See you on the streets!
